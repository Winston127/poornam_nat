Our Story: Pure Tradition, Modern Convenience

The Purity of Our Oils

We produce our oils using a traditional wooden press (mara-chekku). Our press is unique, crafted from a Vagai wood pestle and a stone mortar. This "cold-press" method generates no heat. This is vital because it locks in 100% of the natural nutrients and vitamins from the seeds, giving your body only the best.

The Goodness of Our Rice

We believe in the power of our heritage rice, but we also know your time is precious. Traditionally, these rices require 6 hours of soaking. We've solved that. Our Ready Mix Items let you skip the long wait and prepare a healthy, traditional meal in just 15 minutes. At Pooranam, we make healthy eating simple, fast, and authentic.
