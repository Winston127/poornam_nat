Our Process

All the oils we produce are extracted using traditional wood-pressed machines. Our setup features a pestle made from vaagai wood and a stone mortar, ensuring that the oil is extracted without generating heat. This method preserves the full nutritional value of the seeds, making the oils highly beneficial for health.

We are also deeply committed to promoting our heritage rice varieties, which are rich in unique qualities and nutritional benefits. Our passion for sharing these traditional grains and their goodness with everyone is what inspired us to enter the food production space.

Typically, these rice varieties require soaking for at least six hours before cooking. To make things easier, our Ready Mix Items allow you to prepare meals in just 15 minutes—no soaking needed. All our products are designed to be quick and simple to cook, making traditional nutrition accessible for modern lifestyles.
